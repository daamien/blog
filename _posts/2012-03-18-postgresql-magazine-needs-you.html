--- layout: post title: PostgreSQL Magazine needs you ! ---

The first official issue of PostgreSQL Magazine is on its way !

If you want to help us, now is the time ! We need reviewers. Proof-reading takes only a few minutes and it's a great way of contributing. It's an important job that gives the magazine its quality.

The review process based on a quite simple wiki. Each article is a wiki page. As a reviewer you just have to click "edit" and correct as many errors you can spot :-)

There are currently 4 articles waiting for review and few more to come by the end of the month.

If you're interested, please read the reviewer guidelines and subscribe to the pgmag mailing list.

We plan to release PostgreSQL Magazine #01 in April. And second issue is already scheduled for october 2012. Stay tuned !

For more details, please check : http://pgmag.org/
